India vs Afghanistan

HITman show


Rohit slams record World Cup ton as India crush Afghanistan by 8 wickets. Rohit Sharma oozed class and elegance en route to a recording-breaking hundred, leading India to an emphatic eight-wicket win over Afghanistan in the ODI World Cup. Afghanistan came as the underdogs in the tournament but they managed to score a fighting total of 272 against the quality bowling line up. In the chase India had a good start.



Venue: Arun Jaitley Stadium, Delhi





  • Afghanistan captain Hastmatullah Shahidi lead the team by scoring brilliant 80
  • India's Top bowler clinched with 4 Wickets
  • India's Captain Rohit Sharma with opener Ishan kishan sets the stage on fire initially.
  • Rohit didn't gave a chance to the oponent with his innings.
  • Rohit scored 131 and created some records.
  • With this century he became the top batsman with more number of 100's in WC history
  • At last virat Kohli completed the match with unbeaten of 55*


Scores:


AFG - 272/8 (50 overs)

IND - 273/2 (35 overs)



Player of the Match : Rohit Sharma
